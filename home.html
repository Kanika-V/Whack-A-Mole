Welcome to Whack-a-Mole!

Whack-A-Mole is an interactive game aimed at testing your reflexes. Click on the moles that appear on the screen in order to get points. Once you catch the the moles you'll start to rack up a score.

But watch out, you only have 60 seconds! Click "Game" to play! And refresh the page to give it another go!
