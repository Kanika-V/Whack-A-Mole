About Whack-a-Mole

Whack-a-Mole is a fun and interactive game where players try to click on moles that pop up from random holes. It's a test of speed and accuracy, perfect for all ages! Try your best at catching all the moles before they can hide again, here how you play.

This is what the game looks like while playing the mole will randomly pop-up on the screen and the players have to hit it in time.

To check your score look above and you will see a timer and your score

Once the timer runs out this message will display your score
